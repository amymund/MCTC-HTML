Mayhem

Norwegian Black Metal Pioneers

How Mayhem Lived and Breathed Black Metal

  • Members of the band were connected to a series of Norwegian church burnings.
  • Lead Singer "Dead" was the first black-metal musician to wear corpse paint - but beyond this Dead would even bury his stage clothes and dig them up to wear on concert nights.
  • During performances, Dead would often cut himself with hunting knives and broken glass.
  • The band frequently featured pig or sheep heads impaled on stakes at the front of the stage.
  • Dead committed suicide in the house rented by the band. He was found by Euronymous, who discovered him with slit wrists and a shotgun wound to the head. Dead's suicide note began with "Excuse all the blood" ...
  • Upon discovering Dead’s body, Euronymous went to a nearby store, purchased a disposable camera, and photographed the corpse after rearranging certain items.
  • Rumors circulated that Euronymous had made a stew from bits of Dead's brain and fashioned necklaces from fragments of his skull.
  • Hellhammer penned the lyrics to "Pagan Fears" in his own blood in October 2019.
  • On the 10th of August 1993, Vikernes stabbed Euronymous to death. His body was discovered outside the apartment, bearing twenty-three stab wounds—two to the head, five to the neck, and sixteen to the back.
